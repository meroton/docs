# Working at Meroton

Meroton is growing and we're always on the lookout for people who can
contribute. We are a small team of people that are passionate about
efficient development and believe that small improvement for everyone is
are big improvement for organizations as a whole.

## Web developer

We are looking for web developers to join our team. As a web developer
at Meroton you would be working on improving data visualization for
development teams. Working as a developer for other developers having an understanding of how compilations and build systems work are valuable.

We typically work in golang and react with typescript.

Salary after experience, typical range of 50.000€ to 100.000€ anually.

## IT infrastructure engineer

Infrastructure engineers that can help manage our hardware clusters. At
Meroton you would be managing clusters ranging from 1000 to 100.000 cpus
and a strong understanding of insfrastructure automation is a necessity.

Typical skills we look for are:

- Strong linux fundamentals
- Terraform
- Kubernetes
- Knowledge about networking, virtual and physical
- Experience working with cloud environment
- Experience setting up rack mounted hardware

Salary after experience, typical range of 50.000€ to 100.000€ anually.

## Build your own role

At Meroton we work holistically with everything that is
required to make development efficient. That means that what we do is
not limited to a specific language or technology, rather around any
subject that allows findings solutions to large scale development
problems.

That said, to get some insight into what we work with, having experience
in this suite of technologies is helpful.

- [git](https://git-scm.com/about/branching-and-merging)
- [bazel](https://bazel.build/)
- [buildbarn](https://github.com/buildbarn)
- [go](https://go.dev/)
- [rust](https://rust-lang.org/)
- [kubernetes](https://kubernetes.io/)
- [react](https://react.dev/)
- [zuul](https://zuul-ci.org/)

Knowledge about compilers and packaging are also a large plus.
